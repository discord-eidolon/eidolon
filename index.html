Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I add My Awesome Discord Bot to my server?

A: It's simple! Just click the "Add to Discord" button at the top or bottom of this page, select your server, and authorize the bot. Make sure you have "Manage Server" permissions on your desired server.

Q: What are the main commands?

A: Our bot uses slash commands (e.g., `/help`, `/mod kick`, `/play`). You can type `/help` in any channel the bot can see to get a full list of commands and their usage.

Q: Is the bot always online?

A: Yes! We strive for 24/7 uptime. In case of any maintenance or unexpected downtime, we will announce it on our support server.

Q: How can I get support or report a bug?

A: Join our official support server! Our team and community members are there to help you with any questions or issues you might encounter.

Invite My Awesome Discord Bot

*To get your bot invite link:
1. Go to Discord Developer Portal: discord.com/developers/applications
2. Select your application.
3. Go to "OAuth2" -> "URL Generator".
4. Select "bot" scope, and any permissions your bot needs.
5. Copy the generated URL and paste it above!
